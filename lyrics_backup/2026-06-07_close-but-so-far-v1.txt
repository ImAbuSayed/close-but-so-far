[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Across the wooden table, our hands almost touch
A fragile line between a whisper and a rush
I feel the pull inside your quiet, heavy stare
We are so close, but still a million miles from there

[Verse 1]
The amber light casts a shadow on your face
I hold my breath to capture every single trace
A sudden secret we are trying not to keep
A slow burning fire waking from a heavy sleep

[Pre-Chorus]
Every second pulling me inside your gravity
The quiet tension taking over all my sanity
I watch the way you let your gentle fingers fall
I want to break the distance, breaking down the wall

[Chorus]
Close but so far, a canyon in a spark
Dancing on the edge of the softly falling dark
I trace your outline without reaching out my hand
Lost inside a feeling that I finally understand

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, inches away, but it feels like a mile
Waiting on the magic of your slow, warm smile

[Verse 2]
You take a breath and let your eyes lock into mine
A sudden shiver running up and down my spine
The background voices fade into the empty air
I am wrapped completely in the perfume that you wear
